SUBSIM Features

  • Estimations of household distributions of subsidies;
  • Estimations of the impact of subsidy reforms on household wellbeing, poverty, inequality and government revenues; 
  • Estimations of the impact of compensatory cash transfers;
  • Estimations of direct and indirect effects;
  • Estimations of behavioral effects;
  • Estimations with linear and non-linear pricing;
  • Estimations with linear and non-linear demand schedules;
  • Estimation of standard errors and confidence intervals based on survey designs;
  • Options for Increasing Block Tariffs (IBT), Volume Differentiated Tariffs (VDT) and mixed systems for non-linear pricing systems;
  • Options for endogenous/exogenous and short-term/long-term indirect effects simulations;
  • Options for cost-push and marginal profit push simulations of indirect effects;
  • Production of automated tables and figures for subsidies profiles;
  • Customized outputs by population groups and other variables;
  • Choice of language (English or French);
  • Unifies syntax and parameter use across various estimation procedures for distributive analysis.
